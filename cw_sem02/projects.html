Sahana Suwaya 1

Duration - starting at 6:00 AM every Sunday.
Anyone can join this.

Sunday Nutritious Meal Program for Elders .


In parallel with the government’s elderly assistance scheme, we provide nutritious lunch meals to senior citizens every Sunday at the Mathugama Post Office premises.
Meal preparation begins at 6:00 AM every Sunday, and volunteers are welcome to join us on-site to contribute their time, effort, or resources.

You can support this initiative by:

  • Assisting with meal preparation and packing.
  • Donating ingredients or cooked meals.
  • Contributing financially to help sustain the program.

Gam Udawa

Duration - starting at 8:00 AM on July 25, 2025.
Anyone can join this.

Ending Hunger Through Home Gardening .


As part of our Zero Hunger initiative, a special workshop titled “Gam Udawa” will be held on July 25, 2025, starting at 8:00 AM at the Divisional Council premises.
This program is open to all individuals passionate about agriculture and self-sufficiency. We warmly invite you to participate and be part of the movement to eliminate hunger in our communities

The workshop will focus on:

  • Raising awareness on effective and sustainable cultivation methods.
  • Distributing free seeds to participants.
  • Promoting home gardening as a long-term food security solution.

Divi Naguma

Duration - on July 30, 2025
Anyone can join this.

Fundraising for Food Aid in Kalutara District. .


Divi Naguma is a fundraising initiative organized to provide essential food assistance to low-income families in the Kalutara District. The program will take place on July 30, 2025, at the Kalutara Temple premises.
Your contribution, no matter the size, will help feed a family in need. Join hands with us to eliminate hunger and uplift the lives of the most vulnerable in our community.
Together, we can bring nourishment and dignity to every home.

There are 2 ways you can support:

  • Make a direct donation to the fundraising campaign.
  • Collect and sell donation tickets issued by the Kalutara Temple and return the funds raised.

SHAKTHI

Duration - Any time
Anyone can join this.

Supporting Low-Income Mothers .

SHAKTHI is a dedicated support program for low-income breastfeeding mothers, providing them with essential nutritious food items and basic necessities to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and child.

This initiative is carried out in collaboration with the Regional Health Services Office and operates under full government endorsement.
We warmly invite you to be a part of this impactful effort.

Your financial contributions or in-kind donations can help uplift the lives of mothers in need and ensure a healthier future for the next generation.

Sisu Saviya

Duration - Any time
Anyone can join this.

Supporting Students Through Meals .

Conducted in collaboration with school canteens, Sisu Saviya provides nutritious daily meals to students from low-income families.

These meals are distributed through the school canteen system, ensuring children receive the nourishment they need to focus, learn, and thrive.
The entire cost of meals is covered by this program, easing the burden on families struggling with food insecurity.

You can also make a meaningful impact by contributing financially. Donation options are provided below to support this vital initiative.
